How Your Rod is Made

From a single 12?foot culm of Tonkin bamboo to a tuned two?piece rod with duplicate tip—here’s the journey.

  1. 1) Split & Straighten

    Culms are flamed, split into strips, and heat?straightened for grain integrity.

  2. 2) Plane & Taper

    Precision planing to final tapers that define the action and recovery.

  3. 3) Bind & Cure

    Sections are glued, bound, and cured, then hand?sanded to spec.

  4. 4) Wrap & Finish

    Silk wraps, varnish, cork shaping, hardware fitment, and final tuning.
